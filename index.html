#HelpUkraine

What's going on?

The war in Ukraine has resulted in a catastrophic humanitarian crisis with 12 million people now in need of lifesaving humanitarian assistance, relief, and protection.

As civilians flee from the warzone, the UN estimates 4 million Ukrainian refugees will need protection and assistance in neighboring countries. In addition, 6.5 million people are internally displaced within Ukraine, and this number is expected to grow.

Below we composed a list of verified humanitarian aid organizations that you can donate today to #HelpUkraine
